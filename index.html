Our Story

BORN ON THE
STREETS OF
KABALAGALA

In Uganda, pool table sports have long been a favorite pastime, especially among urban youth. Yet for too long, the sport has remained informal — with few avenues for growth, recognition, or career development.

CuePointe was founded in the bustling neighborhoods of Kabalagala, Kansanga, and along Ggaba Road, driven by one belief: every player deserves a platform to shine.

PLAYER REGISTRATION

WHY REGISTER?

Joining CuePointe gives you access to structured tournaments, an official player profile, digital rankings, and a community of serious cue-sports players across Kampala.

Whether you're a casual player or a serious competitor, there's a place for you in the CuePointe ecosystem.

  • 1
    Fill the Form

    Provide your basic info and select your skill level and preferred tournament.

  • 2
    Get Confirmed

    We'll contact you via WhatsApp or phone to confirm your spot and provide venue details.

  • 3
    Pay Entry Fee

    Entry fees are collected on the day of the event at the venue. No online payment required.

  • 4
    Play & Earn Rankings

    Every game contributes to your digital profile and official CuePointe player ranking.
