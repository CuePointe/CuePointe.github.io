HOW IT WORKS

Registering with CuePointe gets you into the official tournament system. Every match you play counts toward your ranking profile.

  • 1
    Fill the Form

    Choose your skill level, preferred tournament, and provide your contact details.

  • 2
    WhatsApp Confirmation

    We'll message you within 24 hours to confirm your spot and send venue details.

  • 3
    Pay at the Venue

    Entry fees are paid on the day. No online payment required.

  • 4
    Play & Earn Your Rank

    Every game earns ranking points. Your official CuePointe profile is activated after your first game.
