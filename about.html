Who We Are

BORN ON THE STREETS
OF KABALAGALA

In Uganda, pool table sports have long been a favourite pastime — especially among urban youth. Yet for too long, the sport remained informal, with few avenues for growth, recognition, or career development.

CuePointe was founded in the bustling neighbourhoods of Kabalagala, Kansanga, and along Ggaba Road, driven by one belief: every player deserves a platform to shine.

We have transformed a traditional neighbourhood pastime into a structured, data-driven career path for emerging talent. Our mission is to deliver fair, transparent, and inclusive competitions that promote discipline, sportsmanship, and sustainable opportunities for players and partners alike.
